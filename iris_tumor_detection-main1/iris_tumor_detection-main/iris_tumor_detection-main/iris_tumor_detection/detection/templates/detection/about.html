About the mission

Our project focuses on using advanced machine learning techniques to detect tumors in iris images. We leverage convolutional neural networks (CNN) for precise and reliable results. Our mission is to provide a tool that aids in early diagnosis and improves the efficiency of medical professionals.

We aim to combine cutting-edge technology with user-friendly interfaces to make medical imaging analysis accessible and effective.
